    reflections on ungrading

    January 5, 2026
    Teaching, Thought

    About a year and a half ago, I discovered Ungrading as a teaching strategy for higher education. During that time, I implemented the strategy in all of my four classes, in various ways, using methods shared by several proponents of the ungrading scheme: Robert Talbert, Jesse Stommel, etc. I’ve also added a few “innovations” of my own.

    Over the last year and a half since implementing the strategy, I’ve noticed that some methods work better than others. Here, I’ll share some of my experiences. First, I’ll cover some of the interventions made in the classroom, and then I’ll cover how I adjusted our LMS (Canvas) to accommodate.
